MazeCity Comic Con Bangladesh 2024

A public pop-culture convention celebrating anime, manga, comics, gaming, cosplay, and creator communities in Bangladesh. Programming typically features a cosplay runway/contest, artist alley and merch stalls, gaming corners, community panels/meet-ups, dedicated photo zones, and live stage segments for families and young fans.

  • Date: 20–21 December 2024
  • Location: International Convention City Bashundhara (ICCB), Dhaka
  • Role: Design & Operations Support (minor)

What Ash Did

Ash supported the team with display design (signage and layout tweaks for stalls and stage backdrops) and light event operations (runner coordination, queue flow, attendee guidance). Ash also assisted with media management on a minor basis—collecting clips/photos from volunteers, labeling assets, and forwarding selects for same-day social posts.

Event Summary

The convention brought together local creators, vendors, and fan communities for cosplay showcases, artist merchandise, gaming sessions, and interactive activities. Highlights included the main-stage cosplay segment, community meet-ups, and a busy artist alley featuring prints, badges, and handcrafted items. The focus was inclusive participation and creator visibility, with ample opportunities for photos and short-form content capture.
