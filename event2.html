Summersoul Season 2 — Dota 2 Tournament

Online competitive Dota 2 tournament for Bangladesh-based stacks and campus teams. The goal was to deliver a fair, time-bound schedule with clear verification and Discord-based communication.

  • Prize Pool: 20,000 BDT
  • Slots: 16 Teams
  • Mode: Captains Mode
  • Match Timings: 10:00 PM–12:00 AM (Bangladesh Time)
  • Registration Period: 19–26 September 2025
  • Location: Online — Discord coordination
  • Role: Tournament Management, Game Hosting, Event Planning, Post Design Support

Registration: Google Form  |  Discord: Join Server

Team Requirements & Rules

  • Team of 5 players (optional 1 substitute).
  • MMR Cap: total team MMR ≤ 22,500. If a substitute is added, only the top five MMRs count toward the cap.
  • Immortal players must have calibrated accounts (uncalibrated Immortal accounts not allowed).
  • Other ranks: calibration strongly advised before registering.
  • Verification: all players are contacted after registration for account checks.
  • No smurfing: secondary accounts lead to disqualification.
  • Registration Fee: 500 BDT per team (registration is complete only after payment).
  • All registered teams must join the official Discord for schedules and coordination.

Tournament Format

Group Stage leading into an Elimination Bracket. All matches are played in Captains Mode. Admins scheduled fixtures within the nightly 10:00 PM–12:00 AM window to minimize clashes with other tournaments.
