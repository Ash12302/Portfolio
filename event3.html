Dhaka Comic Con

Pop-culture convention in Dhaka celebrating comics, anime, games, cosplay, and local creators. Programming typically includes artist booths, vendor stalls, cosplay showcases, gaming corners, photo ops, and stage segments for panels and meet-ups.

  • Date: 12–13 January 2024
  • Location: Bangabandhu International Conference Center (BICC), Dhaka
  • Official Page: facebook.com/DhakaComicon
  • Role: Stall Art & Cards (Canva) — minor support

What Ash Did

Ash provided light design support using Canva—creating stall artwork and branded cards/handouts used at the booth. Scope was limited but ensured clean, readable layouts, consistent colors, and print-ready files sized to vendor specs.

Event Summary

The convention gathered artists, vendors, and fans for cosplay, signings, merchandise, and community activities. Traffic peaks aligned with stage shows and cosplay hours; booth materials focused on quick recognition and clear calls to action for social follow-ups.
